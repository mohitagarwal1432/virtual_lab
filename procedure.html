PROCEDURE

SIMULATION PROCEDURE

  1. STEP 1: Simulation starts with choosing the test which is tension or compression test.

  2. STEP 2: Measure the Outer-Diameter of the spring by the help of vernier calliper.

  3. STEP 3: Measure the Inner-Diameter of the spring by the help of vernier calliper.

  4. STEP 4: Fix the spring in the spring testing machine

  5. STEP 5: Rotate the pressure valve of the spring testing machine

  6. STEP 6:Apply the load on the spring testing machine.

  7. STEP 7: Apply the load on the spring testing machine to observe the deflection. The dial shows the applied load.

  8. STEP 8: Calculation and results.

PREACAUTION

  1. Keep the machine protected from dust and dirt.
  2. Do not overload equipment.
  3. Level the test machine before starting using levelling screw.
  4. Use machine manually in case of power failure and avoid use of electric operation of case of low or high voltage for safety of electric motor and other electric gadgets.
